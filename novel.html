The Chronicles of Kretos

A tale of fractured worlds and heroes reborn

Prologue: The Sundering

In the age before memory, when Order and Chaos waged war across the cosmos, Kretos was born from their final, cataclysmic clash. The Primordial Deity of Life and Death, seeing the world about to shatter, wove a final seal—binding the warring forces into the very fabric of reality.

Now, millennia later, the seal weakens. Heroes rise. Monsters stir. And the fate of Kretos hangs in the balance...
